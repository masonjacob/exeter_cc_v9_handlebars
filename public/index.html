Who We Are

Located in the tranquil country-side of Exeter, Rhode Island, The Exeter Country Club offers a premier golf experience like no other. Long considered one of Rhode Island's finest championship golf courses, ECC is accessible to the public and provides an enjoyable challenge for players of all abilities.

With rolling lush fairways, scenic views, carefully manicured greens, and a country setting, The ECC provides a relaxing golf experience. Whether you're looking for a casual round with your family, or a competitive match with the gang, you'll find ECC has what it takes to satisfy all your golfing needs.

The ECC is a proud member of the Rhode Island Golf Association (RIGA) and the United States Golf Association (USGA).

History

Designed by renowned architect Geoffrey Cornish, the Exeter Country Club was built in 1969 by the Hendrick family, who continue to operate the course to this day.
